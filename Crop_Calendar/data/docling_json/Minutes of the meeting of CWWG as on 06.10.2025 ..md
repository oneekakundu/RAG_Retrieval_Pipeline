## Key Insights

1. Kharif Sowing Status: As on 03 October, 2025, the area sown under Kharif crops is 1121.46 lakh ha as compared to 1114.95 lakh ha during the corresponding period of last year. About 102.3 percent of the normal area has been sown under the Kharif season crops. The status of sowing under Rice, Pulses, Coarse Cereals and Sugarcane is better than in the same period of last year.

## 2. Weather Update :

- a) Temperature: Maximum temperatures is likely to be below normal in most of India except some parts of Tamil Nadu and South Karnataka.
- b) Rainfall Distribution: From October 01 -October 05 2025, the All-India cumulative rainfall is  58%  higher  than  its  Long  Period  Average  (LPA),  with  regional  variations:  (i)  East  &amp; Northeast  India:  136%;  (ii)  Northwest  India:  192%;  (iii)  Central  India:  62%;  and  South Peninsula: (-) 50%.
- Heavy to very heavy rainfall with possibility of extremely heavy rainfall likely over Sub Himalayan West Bengal &amp; Sikkim and Meghalaya today, the 05 th  October, 2025.
- A fresh intense western disturbance is likely cause heavy to very heavy rainfall spell over Northwest India during 05 th  - 07 th  October with peak intensity on 06 th  October.
- Isolated  extremely  heavy  rainfall  ( ≥ 21  cm)  likely  over  Sub-Himalayan  West  Bengal  &amp; Sikkim on 05 th  October.
- Isolated heavy to very heavy rainfall over Bihar on 05 th  and heavy rainfall over Gangetic West Bengal on 05 th  and Sub-Himalayan West Bengal &amp; Sikkim on 06 th  October.
- Light to moderate rainfall/thunderstorm at most/many places with isolated thunderstorm  &amp;  gusty  winds  very  likely  over  East  India  during  next  3-4  days.  Fairly widespread to widespread rainfall activity is likely over Northeast India.
- Isolated to scattered rainfall activity likely over West &amp; East India.

## Withdrawal of Southwest Monsoon 2025

- Southwest  monsoon  has  withdrawal  from  many  parts  of  Gujarat  and  Rajasthan;  entire Punjab and Haryana, Chandigarh &amp; Delhi; some parts of Madhya Pradesh, UP, Himachal Pradesh and Jammu &amp; Kashmir till now.

- Conditions are favourable for further withdrawal of southwest monsoon from some more parts of Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh and some parts of Maharashtra during next 45 days.
