## Key Insights

1. Rabi  Sowing  Status: Rabi  sowing  data  collection  has  been  started  and  the  sowing  is  in preliminary stage. As on October 31, 2025, the area sown under Rabi crops is 75.77 lakh ha (including 41.69 lakh  ha in Rapeseed &amp; Mustard) as compared to 65.88 lakh ha during the corresponding period of last year.
- As on 31.10.2025,  37.79%  of  area  has  been  harvested  all  over  India.  Approximately  59.83% area of Pulses mainly in Urdbean (64%), Moongbean (93%) &amp; Mothbean (100%); 55.71% area of Oilseeds majorly in Groundnut (54.48%) &amp; Sesamum (60.09%); 59.54% area of Shri Anna cum Coarse Cereals majorly in Jowar (67.54%)  &amp; Bajra (86.53%) and 28% area of Rice has been harvested for kharif crops 2025-26.

## 2. Weather Update :

- a) Temperature: Minimum temperature is likely to be above normal over North East India, East India, Central India, Gujarat and many parts of North West India.
- b) Rainfall  Distribution: From  October  01 -November  02  2025,  the  All-India  cumulative rainfall  is  49%  higher  than  its  Long  Period Average  (LPA),  with regional variations:  (i) East  &amp;  Northeast  India:  20%;  (ii)  Northwest  India:  158%;  (iii)  Central  India:  71%;  and South Peninsula: 32%.
- A fresh Western Disturbance is likely to influence Western Himalayan region from 4th November, 2025.
- Formation of a low-pressure area over eastcentral Bay of Bengal &amp; adjoining Myanmar coast, under its influence heavy rainfall at isolated places likely over Andaman &amp; Nicobar Islands today, the 02 nd  November, 2025.
- Above normal rainfall is likely in East India, NE India, Gujarat, East Rajasthan, West M.P., Vidarbha and U.P. Below normal rainfall is likely in South India.
- Isolated to scattered rainfall activity likely over Eastern and Southern India.

## 3. Inputs Situation

- a) Water Reservoir Status : As on 30 October 2025, the total live water storage in 161 important reservoirs across the country (monitored by CWC) is 165.782 billion cubic meter, which is 90.85% of total live storage capacity (182.479 BCM). The current year's storage is nearly 104.4 percent of last year's storage and 116.3 percent of the average storage of the last ten years  during  the  corresponding  period.  The  overall  storage  position  is  better  than  the corresponding period of last year in the country as a whole and is also better than the Normal storage during the corresponding period.

(as on 03.11.2025)

<!-- image -->

- b) Groundwater  Levels :  In  August  2025,  around  89%  of  the  country's  monitoring  stations exhibited a depth to water level within 10 meters below ground level. Deeper water levels of more than 20 m are observed in around 5% of stations of the country covering mainly the western states, especially Western Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh.
- The deeper groundwater level in the states like Western Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh during August 2025 has been significantly influenced by the over extraction of groundwater compared to the recharge during the monsoon period.
- Annual water level comparison with the previous year August 2024 to August 2025 has shown  that  about  56.5%  of  total  analyzed  stations  of  the  country  experienced  rise  in annual water level fluctuation because of the higher rainfall in 2025 compared to 2024 in Monsoon period.
- About 70% of the analyzed stations of the unconfined aquifer experienced a rise of water level  in  the  decadal  mean  water  level  fluctuation  of  2015-2024  with  respect  to  August 2025.
- c) Seeds  and  Fertilizers :  For  Kharif  2025,  certified  seed  availability  is  178.64  lakh  quintals against a requirement of 164.05 lakh quintals, ensuring a surplus of 14.60 lakh quintals. The fertilizer supply remains stable across all products.
