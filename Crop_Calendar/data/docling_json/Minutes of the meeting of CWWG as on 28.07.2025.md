## Key Insights

1. Kharif Sowing Status: As on 25 July, 2025, the area sown under Kharif crops is 829.64 lakh ha (including 55.16 lakh ha (105% of normal area) in Sugarcane) as compared to 797.91 lakh ha during the corresponding period of last year. About 75.6 percent of the normal area has been sown  under  the  Kharif  season  crops.  The  status  of  sowing  under  Rice,  Pulses  and  Coarse Cereals is better than in the same period of last year.

Heavy  to  very  heavy  rainfall  likely  to  continue  over  Arunachal  Pradesh,  Assam  and Meghalaya during next 7 days.

## Inputs Situation

3. Water Reservoir Status :  As on 24 th  July 2025, the total live water storage in 161 important reservoirs across the country (monitored by CWC) is 110.86 Billion Cubic Meter, which is 61% of total live storage capacity (182.46 BCM). The current year's storage is nearly 159 percent of last year's storage and 153 percent of the average storage of the last ten years during  the  corresponding  period.  The  overall  storage  position  is  better than  the corresponding period of last year in the country as a whole and is also better than the Normal storage during the corresponding period.
