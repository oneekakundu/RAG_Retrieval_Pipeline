## Key Insights

1. Kharif Sowing Status: As on 26 September, 2025, the area sown under Kharif crops is 1120.73 lakh ha as compared to 1113.72 lakh ha during the corresponding period of last year. About 102.2 percent of the normal area has been sown under the Kharif season crops. The status of sowing under Rice, Pulses, Coarse Cereals and Sugarcane is better than in the same period of last year.

## 2. Weather Update :

- a) Temperature: Maximum temperatures is likely to be above normal in NW India, Bihar, NE India and Gujarat.
- b) Rainfall Distribution: From June 01 -Sept 28 2025, the All-India cumulative rainfall is 8% higher than its Long Period Average (LPA), with regional variations: (i) East &amp; Northeast India:(-)20%; (ii) Northwest India: 28%; (iii) Central India: 14%; and South Peninsula: 11%.
- Heavy to very heavy rainfall likely at some places over Konkan, Madhya Maharashtra, Gujarat  state  during  28 th   September  -  01 st   October.  Isolated  extremely  heavy  rainfall  is also likely over north Konkan (including Mumbai), north Madhya Maharashtra and south Gujarat Region on 28th; Saurashtra &amp; Kutch on 29 th  September.
- Thunderstorm with lightning very likely over West Rajasthan on 29 th  &amp; 30 th  September and East Rajasthan during next 5 days.
- Isolated to scattered rainfall activity likely over many parts of the country except Gangetic Plains,  East  Rajasthan,  Madhya Pradesh, West &amp; Central India where fairy widespread rainfall activity is likely.
- Rainfall is likely to be normal to above normal over most parts of West India, many parts of northwest and adjoining western Himalayan region &amp; central India and along the West Coast. Below normal rainfall is likely in many parts of Northeast &amp; adjoining East India and South Peninsular India.
