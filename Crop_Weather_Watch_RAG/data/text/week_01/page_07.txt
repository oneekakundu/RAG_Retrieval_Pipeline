4. REMOTE SENSING BASED SOIL MOISTURE OBSERVATION 
 
(a) Soil Moisture Observation  
 
Soil moisture during 07 June – 13 June 2025 was better than the average of the past 9 years 
in Gujarat, Rajasthan, Western M.P, Maharashtra, Karnataka, A.P., Telangana, Tamil Nadu, 
Odisha, Jharkhand and West Bengal. 
 
It was lower than average of past 9 years in Bihar and Eastern UP.
